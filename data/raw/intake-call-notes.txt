Intake call — Tidalwave / Senior Full-Stack Engineer
On call: Priya (CEO), Marcus (Head of Eng), me
~June 6

- so they're 22 people, raised ~14M series A last year. building observability for AI
  agents, very technical product, customers are developers
- Marcus owns this hire. they have 6 engineers now, want to get to ~10 by EOY
- this role: "senior full stack" but honestly Marcus kept saying he cares less about the
  title and more about whether someone can "just go own a surface area without me babysitting"

  ingestion stuff. react. they're on GCP
- must have: 5+ yrs, strong TS, a real backend language. has SHIPPED and OWNED something end

  dev tools or infra background, comfortable with ambiguity

  freezes without a PM and a spec

  equity but not really base
- location: SF hybrid 2 days, or remote US is fine. Marcus mildly prefers SF
- HARD constraint: cannot do visa sponsorship right now. they looked into it, not feasible
  this year. so US work auth required, no sponsorship
- timeline: they're feeling some urgency, want someone in seat ideally within ~6-8 weeks.
  Marcus said the eng team is underwater
- one more thing — Priya said please don't send "resume keyword matches", she wants to
  understand why each person would actually thrive HERE specifically
